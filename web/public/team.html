Our story

Why we built this.

It started with one bad Friday. A PR got merged without review. It touched the payment API. By the time we noticed, 600 transactions had failed. It took 4 hours to roll back. The post-mortem answer? "The diff looked fine."

"Fine" wasn't good enough. No one had told us that changing that payment function would cascade through 3 downstream services. No one had mapped the blast radius. We built Grassion so that conversation would never happen again — for us or for anyone.

What we believe

Our values.

Speed is non-negotiable

Engineers move fast. We can't slow them down. Grassion adds zero friction — analysis in under a second, zero config, zero ceremony. If we slow you down, we've failed.

Transparency builds trust

Every block is explained. Every risk score is justified. Every alert tells you exactly what changed and why it matters. We don't do mystery alerts. Developers deserve to understand their tools.

Business risk, not just code risk

Linters catch syntax. Tests catch bugs. Grassion catches consequences. We're the only tool that maps from code change to business impact — revenue at risk, SLA exposure, customer blast radius.

Built for Indian engineering teams

INR pricing. Indian support hours. Deep understanding of Indian SaaS and FinTech regulatory reality. Built in Odisha with the world in mind — not the other way around.
