Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data. Last updated: April 2026.

At Grassion, your privacy is not a checkbox. It's a core principle. We only collect what we need, we never sell it, and we protect it like it's our own.

1. What We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.

GitHub Repository Data

When you connect a repository, we receive GitHub webhook events for Pull Requests opened against that repo. This includes PR title, description, diff content, author username, and timestamps. We do not access your full repository, read your entire codebase, or store anything beyond the PR event payload.

Usage Data

We log which features you use, how often PRs are analyzed, and error logs. This helps us improve the product. We do not track individual keystrokes or screen recordings.

Payment Information

All payments are processed by Razorpay. We never see or store your full card number. We only receive a payment confirmation token and subscription status from Razorpay.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze PR events and generate AI risk scores using OpenAI's API
  • To send you email notifications about your account and PR events (only if enabled)
  • To process subscription payments and manage your plan
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve our AI models and product features (using aggregated, anonymised data only)

3. OpenAI and Third-Party AI Processing

PR diffs are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis. This means your code diffs pass through OpenAI's infrastructure. We send only the diff content and repository name — never your full codebase, credentials, or user data. OpenAI processes these as transient API calls under their data processing agreement. We do not use your data to train OpenAI models.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We share data only with:

  • OpenAI — for PR analysis (diff content only)
  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Railway — our cloud infrastructure provider who hosts and stores your data in India/Singapore

5. Data Retention

PR event data is retained for 12 months by default. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings. Account deletion is permanent and processed within 30 days.

6. Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Authentication uses JWT tokens with secure HTTP-only cookies. We perform regular security reviews and follow OWASP best practices.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Email us at privacy@grassion.com and we will respond within 7 business days.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify you by email and update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Grassion after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@grassion.com or write to us at Grassion, Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India.
