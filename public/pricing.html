Frequently asked questions

What does "Continuous Protection" mean?
Continuous Protection watches your GitHub repo 24/7. Every time you push a commit, Grassion automatically scans the changed files. If it finds an unprotected endpoint, it raises a fix PR immediately — without you doing anything.
What frameworks does Grassion support?
Currently Grassion works best with Node.js/Express applications. Support for Django, FastAPI, Rails, and Laravel is coming soon.
Is my code stored anywhere?
No. Grassion reads your code temporarily to analyze it, but never stores your source code on our servers. Only scan metadata and results are stored.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your Pro features remain active until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets through Razorpay.
